JOVAN STOJKOVIĆ (JOHNOWAN) IS A MULTIMEDIA ARTIST BASED IN BELGRADE,SERBIA. MAIN FIELDS JOHNOWAN WORKS IN ARE MUSIC (WRITING MUSIC AND LYRICS, PRODUCING, RECORDING, SINGING),GRAPHIC DESIGN (VISUAL IDENTITY, BRANDING, GRAPHICS, ILLUSTRATIONS ETC.), AND SOME PHOTOGRAPHY AND 2D ANIMATIONS HERE AND THERE.

BEING BORN IN NIŠ, SERBIA, AND HAVING TO MOVE TO BELGRADE AT A VERY YOUNG AGE, HE STARTED EXPLORING HIS CREATIVITY WITH DRAWING AND SINGING. DURING ELEMENTARY SCHOOL DAYS, HE ATTENDED A MUSIC SCHOOL, AND PIANO WAS HIS INSTRUMENT OF CHOICE. IN HIGH SCHOOL, HE STARTED DRAWING HYPERREALISTIC PORTRAITS AND BEGAN LEARNING ABOUT MUSIC PRODUCTION.

DURING THIS PERIOD OF HIS LIFE, HE DECIDED TO PURSUE ARTS, HOPING FOR MUSIC TO BECOME HIS PROFESSION AS A PRODUCER OR A RECORDING ARTIST.

CURRENTLY, JOHNOWAN IS STUDYING DIGITAL ARTS AT THE FACULTY OF MEDIAS AND COMMUNICATIONS. EVER SINCE ENROLLING, HIS ART BEGAN TAKING ON A NEW FORM AND IT BECAME A LOT MORE PROFESSIONAL. HE STARTED RELEASING ORIGINAL MUSIC UNDER THE NAME OF JOHNOWAN. RIGHT NOW HIS FOCUS IS ON CREATING HIS DEBUT ALBUM WHICH CAN BE EXPECTED IN LATE 2024. THE SOUND OF THIS, FOR NOW UNTITLED, PROJECT WILL BE REMINISCENT OF “takvomie doba” AND “nemoj da me lažeš”, HIS LATEST RELEASES.
